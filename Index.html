Advanced HDPE Pipe Manufacturing Process

Our state-of-the-art extrusion technology ensures consistent quality, optimal material properties, and dimensional accuracy in every pipe.

High-Grade Raw Material Selection

We source only PE100 grade polyethylene from certified global suppliers. Each batch undergoes incoming QC for melt flow index, density, and molecular weight distribution before production.

  • PE100 grade virgin material
  • Optimal molecular weight distribution
  • UV stabilizers and antioxidants compounded
  • Batch-certified material traceability

Precision Extrusion Process

PE100 granules are fed into single-screw extruders operating at precise temperature profiles (160-230C). The die head is machined to produce consistent wall thickness and uniform pipe geometry.

  • Temperature-controlled barrel zones
  • Optimized screw geometry for HDPE
  • Die swell compensation calibration
  • Real-time melt pressure monitoring

Controlled Cooling System

Extruded pipes pass through multi-stage water cooling tanks. Precise temperature gradients prevent warping and residual stress while locking in dimensional properties.

  • Multi-zone water spray cooling
  • Temperature gradient optimization
  • Internal air cooling for large diameters
  • Stress-free solidification control

Vacuum Sizing and Calibration

Vacuum sizing tanks maintain precise outer diameter while internal pressure ensures perfect roundness and consistent wall thickness throughout the entire pipe length.

  • Vacuum calibration sleeves
  • Internal air pressure control
  • Online diameter laser measurement
  • Ovality and wall thickness verification

Rigorous Quality Control

Every batch is tested to IS 5984, ISO 4427, and ASTM D3035 standards. Online and offline testing ensures dimensional accuracy, burst pressure, and material properties all meet specification.

  • Hydrostatic pressure testing
  • Melt flow rate verification
  • Impact and tensile strength testing
  • Third-party certification audits

Inkjet Pipe Marking

Pipes are marked with complete identification data using UV-resistant inkjet printing, compliant with IS and ISO marking requirements for full supply chain traceability.

  • Manufacturer name and logo
  • Pipe size, SDR, and pressure rating
  • Material grade and certification marks
  • Batch number for full traceability

Precision Cutting and Length Control

Automated planetary cutters deliver clean, square cuts at precise intervals. Length accuracy maintained to +-10mm across all production runs via encoder-based measurement.

  • Automated planetary pipe cutter
  • Burr-free cut quality
  • Length encoder measurement
  • Auto-reject for non-conforming lengths

Protective Packaging and Dispatch

Finished pipes are bundled, capped, and wrapped to protect against UV, dust, and mechanical damage. Coils are wound on reels for smaller diameters.

  • End caps for bore protection
  • HDPE strap bundling for straight pipes
  • Coil winding for pipes up to 110mm
  • Pallet wrapping for safe transport
